## Musive (Personal Project)

- [Summary] A system that selects and sends mails interesting news among music-related news to subscribers every morning
- [Main Features] article crawling with RSS, article screening with AI
- [Tech Stack] Monorepo, Firebase, OpenAi Api, NodeJS
- [Links] https://github.com/Seungmo-Ku/musive (Repo)
